# Pathlight AI

Pathlight AI is an AI-powered career equity platform designed to help students unlock and advance their career opportunities regardless of background, experience, or access to professional networks.

Built for the Microsoft Azure OpenAI Hackathon, the platform uses AI to create personalised, accessible career guidance that helps students discover hidden strengths, improve employability, connect with mentors, prepare for interviews, and gain visibility in graduate recruitment.

## Core Features

### AI Career Twin

Creates a personalised AI-powered career profile by analysing the user’s CV, LinkedIn, GitHub, goals, and experience to provide tailored recommendations, roadmaps, and opportunities.

### Invisible Potential Scanner

Transforms real-life experiences into recruiter-recognised skills and identifies hidden strengths often overlooked in traditional recruitment processes.

### Blind Potential Recruitment

Supports fairer hiring by anonymising student profiles and helping recruiters discover candidates based on skills, growth potential, and adaptability rather than background or privilege.

### Future Path Simulator

Uses AI to generate multiple personalised career pathways and step-by-step growth plans based on the student’s goals and current position.

### Interview Confidence AI

Provides AI-powered interview analysis with confidence scoring, filler-word detection, communication feedback, and personalised coaching.

### Opportunity Radar

Recommends relevant hackathons, networking events, certifications, volunteering opportunities, and career experiences tailored to the student’s goals and development areas.

## Technologies Used

* Python
* Flask
* Azure OpenAI
* HTML/CSS/JavaScript
* GitHub API Integration
* LinkedIn-inspired networking system
* AI-driven NLP and recommendation systems

## Vision

Pathlight AI aims to help students be seen for their potential, not their privilege.

By combining AI-driven personalisation with inclusive recruitment and mentorship support, the platform works to reduce opportunity gaps and create fairer pathways into industry for the next generation of diverse talent.
